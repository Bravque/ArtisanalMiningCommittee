About Us

The Migori Mining Initiative is a community-centered organization committed to supporting artisanal miners in Migori and neighboring regions. Our mission is to advance sustainable mining practices by equipping miners with the essential knowledge, tools, and resources they need to operate safely and responsibly. We pride ourselves on adapting to the evolving mining landscape and taking a holistic approach to foster growth and sustainability in the local mining sector.

Who We Are

We offer a wide range of services, including safety training, environmental management, access to funding, legal support, health services, and market connections. Each of these services is designed to work together, creating a comprehensive strategy that promotes sustainable practices, improves miners' livelihoods, and strengthens the local mining industry. Our goal is to ensure artisanal miners have the tools and resources they need to succeed, while fostering responsible mining practices for long-term community growth.

Our Success

  • Our Artisanal Mining Committee has successfully trained over 25 miners in safe and sustainable mining practices over the past 6 months. By 2025, we aim to introduce advanced training programs that will develop skilled specialists and leaders within the artisanal mining sector.

  • For the past three years, we have implemented strategies that promote responsible mining, improve safety standards, and enhance livelihoods for many miners in the region.

  • Through our dedication to our core values, we strive to deliver impactful services to artisanal miners, ensuring sustainable growth and community development.

Our Mission

To empower local miners in Migori by fostering resilience and dignity in the face of challenges, while promoting safer, more sustainable practices that reduce exploitation and improve livelihoods.
